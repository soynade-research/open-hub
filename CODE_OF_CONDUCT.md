# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment
Soynade Research is committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity, experience level, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity.

## Our Standards

### Expected Behavior
- Be respectful and considerate
- Use welcoming and inclusive language
- Accept constructive criticism gracefully
- Focus on what's best for the community
- Show empathy toward others

### Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination
- Offensive comments related to identity or background
- Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments
- Publishing others' private information
- Any conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

### Reporting
If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it to:
- **Email:** contact@soynade.ai
- **Subject line:** "Code of Conduct Report"

All reports will be kept confidential.

### Response Timeline
- **Acknowledgment:** Within 1-2 days
- **Investigation:** Within 3-5 days
- **Decision:** Within 7 days

### Consequences
Depending on severity, violations may result in:

1. **Correction:** Private conversation and request to stop
2. **Warning:** Formal warning with documented consequences
3. **Temporary Ban:** Temporary removal from community spaces
4. **Permanent Ban:** Permanent removal from all community spaces

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces (GitHub, Discord, email, events) and when representing the project publicly.
